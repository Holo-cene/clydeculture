## Why this matters

`events.doors_at` and `external_events.doors_at` exist in the schema (v5 change [39])
for exactly the "Doors 7pm, Show 8pm" display case that is common at Glasgow live
music venues. The `RawEvent` interface in `docs/CONNECTOR_GUIDE.md` has `startAt` but
no `doorsAt`. Ticketmaster returns `dates.doorOpenTime` on every event. Without a
field in the interface, connector authors have nowhere to put it, and it will be
buried in the `raw` JSON payload and never surface to the canonical event.
